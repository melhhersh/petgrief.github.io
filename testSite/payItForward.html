One of the things that makes us human is the ability to help others. I believe in Tikkun Olam - A Jewish concept defined by acts of kindness performed to perfect or repair the world.

I offer my services on a sliding scale basis, as my goal is to make sure that everyone that needs to train can afford to do so. I also believe in paying it forward - so if you have some extra time or money or resources, please support these wonderful organizations -

  • PAWs Shelter - https://pawsshelter.org/
  • Blue Dog Rescue - https://www.bluedogrescue.com/
